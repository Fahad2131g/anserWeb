3D Animator

Ultrolon is hiring 3D Animator.

Description

We are seeking a talented and creative 3D Animator to join our team at Ultrolon Studio. The ideal candidate will have a passion for bringing characters, environments, and objects to life through animation. As a 3D Animator, you will work closely with our art directors, modelers, and game developers to create stunning animations that enhance our projects. This role requires a deep understanding of motion, timing, and the principles of animation, as well as a strong technical skill set in Blender.

Position Overview

This opportunity is open to artists residing anywhere. We provide flexible working arrangements, including on-site, fully remote, or hybrid options.

Position Responsibilities

  • Character Animation: Animate characters and creatures, ensuring fluid and realistic movements that align with the project's style and narrative.

  • Object and Environment Animation: Animate objects and environmental elements to create immersive and dynamic scenes.

  • Rigging: Work with riggers to develop and refine character rigs for animation, ensuring they meet the needs of the project.

  • Collaboration: Work closely with the art and development teams to integrate animations into the project, ensuring consistency and quality across all assets.

  • Motion Capture: Process and refine motion capture data for use in animation, ensuring it fits the artistic direction of the project.

  • Review and Iterate: Participate in feedback sessions, making revisions to animations based on team and client feedback.

Position Requirements

  • Minimum of 6 months of experience in 3D Animation.

  • Completion of relevant courses in 3d animation, computer science, or a related field.

  • A degree in Animation, Computer Graphics, or a related field is a plus.

  • Strong proficiency in Blender.

  • Strong understanding of principles of animation.

  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

  • Ability to work collaboratively with a team and adapt to feedback.

  • Passion for game development and creating high-quality gaming experiences through animation.
