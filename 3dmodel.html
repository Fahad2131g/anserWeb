3d Modeler

Ultrolon is hiring 3d modeling artits for exciting projects.

Description

We are seeking a talented and motivated individual with a strong understanding of hard surface modeling. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating detailed and realistic 3D models for game development and animation, and possess the skills to bring our artistic vision to life.

Position Overview

This opportunity is open to artists residing anywhere. We provide flexible working arrangements, including on-site, fully remote, or hybrid options.

Position Responsibilities

  • Create high-quality 3D models of objects such as vehicles, weapons, machinery, and environmental assets for game development and animation projects.

  • Work closely with concept artists and designers to accurately translate 2D concepts into 3D models.

  • Ensure models are optimized for performance and meet technical requirements for various platforms.

  • Collaborate with texture artists to apply detailed textures and materials to models.

  • Implement feedback from art directors and lead artists to refine and enhance 3D models.

  • Troubleshoot and resolve any issues related to 3D modeling throughout the production process.

  • Ensure all models meet quality standards and align with the project's artistic vision.

Position Requirements

  • Minimum of six months of experience in 3D hard surface modeling for game development and animation.

  • Completion of relevant courses in 3D modeling, digital sculpting, or a related field.

  • Degree in Animation, Game Design, Fine Arts, or a related discipline is a plus.

  • Proficiency in using industry-standard software, such as Blender.

  • Strong understanding of hard surface modeling techniques and principles.

  • Ability to create detailed and realistic 3D models from concept art.

  • Knowledge of optimization techniques for game performance.

  • Ability to work collaboratively with a team and adapt to feedback.

  • Passion for hard surface modeling and the game development and animation industry.
