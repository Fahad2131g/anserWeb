Social Media manager and Marketing specialist

Ultrolon is hiring social media manager and marketing specialists for exciting projects.

Description

We are seeking a talented and motivated individual with a strong understanding of social media strategies. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating engaging and impactful social media content and possess the skills to grow and manage our online presence.

Position Overview

This opportunity is open to artists residing anywhere. We provide flexible working arrangements, including on-site, fully remote, or hybrid options.

Position Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement a comprehensive social media strategy to enhance brand awareness and engagement within the game development and animation community.

  • Create, curate, and manage published content (images, video, written) across all social media platforms.

  • Monitor, listen, and respond to users in a "social" way while cultivating leads and sales.

  • Analyze key metrics and tweak strategies as needed to improve social media performance.

  • Collaborate with the game development team to ensure brand consistency and alignment with ongoing projects.

  • Stay up-to-date with the latest social media best practices and technologies.

  • Manage social media advertising campaigns and budgets to maximize ROI.

  • Track and report on social media insights (traffic, engagement, shares, conversion rates).

  • Build and manage relationships with industry influencers, partners, and media.

  • Conduct market research to identify new opportunities and understand customer needs.

Position Requirements

  • Minimum of six months of experience in a related role.

  • Completion of relevant courses in social media management, marketing, or a related field.

  • Degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related discipline is a plus.

  • Strong understanding of social media platforms, tools, and best practices.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

  • Proven ability to create engaging content and manage multiple social media accounts.

  • Analytical skills to interpret social media and marketing metrics.

  • Ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams.

  • Creativity and a passion for the game development industry and animation is a must.
