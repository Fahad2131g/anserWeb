3D texturing artist.

Ultrolon is hiring texturing artists for exciting projects.

Description

We are seeking a talented and motivated individual with a strong understanding of 3D texturing. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating detailed and realistic textures for game development and animation, and possess the skills to bring our artistic vision to life.

Position Overview

This opportunity is open to artists residing anywhere. We provide flexible working arrangements, including on-site, fully remote, or hybrid options.

Position Responsibilities

  • Create high-quality textures for 3D models, including characters, environments, and props, for game development and animation projects.

  • Collaborate with modelers and concept artists to ensure textures accurately represent the intended design and style.

  • Use industry-standard software to paint and apply textures, ensuring they are optimized for performance and visual quality.

  • Develop and implement material shaders that enhance the visual appeal and realism of 3D models.

  • Ensure textures are compatible with various platforms and meet technical requirements.

  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge of texturing techniques, tools, and industry trends.

  • Implement feedback from art directors and lead artists to refine and enhance textures.

Position Requirements

  • Minimum of six months of experience in 3D texturing for game development and animation.

  • Completion of relevant courses in 3D texturing, digital painting, or a related field.

  • Degree in Animation, Game Design, Fine Arts, or a related discipline is a plus.

  • Proficiency in using industry-standard software, such as Substance Painter and Blender.

  • Strong understanding of texturing techniques and principles.

  • Ability to create realistic and stylized textures that enhance the overall visual quality of 3D models.

  • Experience with creating and implementing material shaders.

  • Knowledge of optimization techniques for texture performance.

  • Passion for texturing and the game development industry.
